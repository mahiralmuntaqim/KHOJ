🏆 Feature 5 — Badge System

🏆 Badge System Based on Loyalty

Rewards active and trusted users with badges based on engagement and performance. Badges are awarded automatically when thresholds are reached — first booking, 5 bookings, 10 reviews, and more.

3

UserBadge document created — duplicate prevention via unique index

4

Points Added

Loyalty points added to user account based on badge tier
