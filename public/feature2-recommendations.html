✨ Feature 2 — Smart Recommendations

✨ Smart Recommendation Engine

Suggests relevant services using user behavior, past bookings, and browsing preferences. The more you use KHOJ, the smarter it gets at recommending the right services for you.

📚

Signal 1 — Past Bookings

Categories you've previously booked are weighted higher in future recommendations

👁️

Signal 2 — Browsing Behaviour

Every time you view a category, it's tracked and used to personalize results

⭐

Signal 3 — Top Rated

High-rated services fill any gaps when browsing history is limited

🎯 Get My Recommendations

Personalized recommendations based on your behaviour
🔑 Requires: customer login token

📡 Track Category View

Simulate browsing a category — this trains the recommendation engine

🌟 Popular Services (No Login)

Top-rated services — anyone can see these without signing in
